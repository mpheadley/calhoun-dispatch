# Green Tomato Merch Concepts

This document outlines initial concepts for merchandise for "The Green Tomato," a satirical news project with a deliberately dated 1998-era web aesthetic.

---

### Concept 1: The "Official" Staff Tee

*   **Design:** A simple, left-chest design on a classic heavyweight cotton tee (like a Hanes Beefy-T).
*   **Front:**
    *   Line 1: `The Green Tomato` (in Times New Roman, maybe bolded)
    *   Line 2: `Bureau Chief` (in Times New Roman Italic)
    *   Line 3: `Buford Tatum` (in Comic Sans MS, looking like a bad autograph)
*   **Why it works:** It’s an artifact from Buford’s world. It feels like something he would have ordered from a local print shop in 1999 for the "staff." The font clash is intentional and period-correct.

---

### Concept 2: "The Vegetable of Record"

*   **Design:** A tote bag or a t-shirt with a centered graphic.
*   **Graphic:**
    *   A low-resolution, 8-bit or pixelated rendering of a green tomato (like a clipart from a 90s CD-ROM).
    *   Above the image: `The Green Tomato` (in an 8-bit computer font).
    *   Below the image: `"The Vegetable of Record"` (in Times New Roman, with the quotation marks).
*   **Why it works:** It directly pulls from the paper's tagline. The pixelated art reinforces the "last updated in 1998" vibe.

---

### Concept 3: The "Under Construction" Look

*   **Design:** Uses classic "under construction" animated GIF aesthetics, but as a static print.
*   **Graphic:**
    *   The classic "Under Construction" yellow and black striped barrier.
    *   Maybe the little animated worker digging, but as a still image.
    *   Text: `The Green Tomato: Always Investment-Ready` (in a system font like Arial or Times New Roman).
*   **Why it works:** It's a direct visual quote from the GeoCities/Angelfire era of the web. It connects to the economic development satire mentioned in the concept doc.

---

### Concept 4: The "Guestbook" Hat

*   **Design:** A simple baseball cap (maybe a light-wash denim or khaki color).
*   **Embroidery:**
    *   `Sign My Guestbook` embroidered on the front in a slightly-off-center Comic Sans MS.
*   **Why it works:** It’s a deep cut from the concept doc. Subtle, funny to those who get the joke, and feels like a piece of obscure, forgotten web 1.0 memorabilia.

---

### Concept 5: The "HURT? CALL BERT." Tee

*   **Design:** A parody of a low-budget lawyer ad.
*   **Graphic:**
    *   A grainy, poorly-lit photo of a man who could be "Bert Whitlock."
    *   Text above: `HURT?` (in a dramatic, bold font).
    *   Text below: `CALL BERT.` (in the same font).
    *   Underneath, in smaller print: `Official Sponsor of The Green Tomato` (in Times New Roman).
*   **Why it works:** It brings one of the fictional advertisers from the concept doc to life, making the world of The Green Tomato feel more real and layered.
